Welcome to my page!

A little bit about me and why I made this website:

My spark for music was lit at the age of ten when I saw a movie where this kid about my age played the famous “Smoke On The Water” guitar riff. It blew my mind and ever since I’ve been playing guitar and soon started to compose my own songs and perform them live. That became my greatest passion in life and throughout the years I’ve developed a personal catalog of hundreds of songs, featured on other artist’s songs and produced music for other artists. That’s my musical journey in a nutshell and I’m here looking to produce YOUR music!
